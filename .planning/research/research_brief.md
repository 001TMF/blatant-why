# Research Brief: SARS-CoV-2 RBD for VHH Nanobody Design

**Project:** SARS-CoV-2 RBD VHH Design Campaign
**Research Mode:** Ecosystem Analysis
**Target:** Receptor Binding Domain (RBD) of SARS-CoV-2 Spike Protein
**Application:** VHH Nanobody Design

## Research Scope

### Primary Objectives
1. **Structural Landscape**: High-resolution RBD structures, especially with nanobodies
2. **Epitope Analysis**: Binding sites, hotspots, accessibility mapping
3. **VHH Precedents**: Existing nanobody structures and strategies
4. **Variant Impact**: Conservation analysis across major variants
5. **Design Guidelines**: Actionable insights for VHH targeting

### Key Questions
- Which RBD epitopes are accessible and conserved across variants?
- What VHH binding modes have been successful?
- How do variants affect existing nanobody binding?
- What are the best structural templates for design?

### Deliverable
Comprehensive analysis in `sars_cov2_rbd_analysis.md` with structural insights for VHH design strategy.
